Meet Our Team
Of Seasoned Professionals

Davin A. Bujold II

CEO Of Dwello

Davin A. Bujold is the CEO and current leader of America's top Real Estate firm, Dwello. Davin created Dwello with his best friend Kurnatowski Barrett after they both completed their respective degrees at post secondary institutions. Davin, a Princeton University alumni holds a degree in Molecular Biology. Unfortunately, despite maintaining a perfect 4.0 GPA, he found the work painfully boring, leading him to enter the Real Estate business. His strong leadership skills from his days playing NCAA Division 1 Lacrosse at Princeton has helped form and lead his company from virtually nothing, to massive success on unimaginable levels.

Paul von Kurnatowski Raymond Barrett

CFO and Lead Agent

Kurnatowski Barrett is the CFO and Director of Sales here at Dwello. Kurnatowski graduated high school at just 15 due to his genius level IQ and astonishing grades. He was told he was "too smart" for John Hopkins University, so he was forced to travel farther from his family and attend Harvard. Kurnatowski attended on scholarship where he was named an All-American Rower. By the age of 19 he had finished a degree in Astrophysics and is currently completing a degree in Aerospace Engineering at Cornell University, all while helping lead Dwello. He has also won two gold medals in the Olympics for Team U.S.A in rowing. Truly a national icon.

Cormick Todd Bindleson Kilgannon IV

Lead Sales Agent

Cormick T. Bindleson Kilgannon IV was brought onto the Dwello team as our lead sales agent. If you've bought a house from Dwello, there's no doubt you bought it from Bindleson. Bindleson recieved a degree in Biomedical Science from Notre Dame University. He attended Notre Dame on an athletic scholarship where he was starting Quarterback for the schools football team. Bindleson reached the pinnacle of success, winning the college football championship three years in a row. However, after a crippling loss to The Ohio State University in the 2025 championship game, his love for the game perished. He joined Dwello with his childhood friends and hasn't looked back!
